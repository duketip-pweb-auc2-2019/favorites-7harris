Characters that are in SUPER MARIO BROS




This is Mario. Mario is the default Character that you can play as.


This is Luigi. You can play as him in the co-op mode with Mario.


This is Bowser. He is the Main enemy. You find him in the castles in big castles.


This is Bowser Jr. He is Bowser's son. You find him in smaller castles.


This is a Goomba. He is just an enemy/obsticle you can find in every level. Just jump on him to defeat him.


This is a Thwomp. He likes to smash the ground. Stay out of him way or he will squish you.


This is Koopa Troopa. His shell is very powerful. Jumping on him will put him in his shell.


This is Piranha Plant. He will try and "chomp" you. Stay out of his way.


This is Bullet Bill. He is a large bullet that tries to hit you. Jump on top of it to destroy it.


This is Boo. When you aren't looking at him he glides toward you. You find him in haunted houses.
